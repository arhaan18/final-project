Future Plans

Utkal Automobiles (body building division) has been in operation since 1969 and is now a well known and reputed brand with a unparallel reputation for quality. Our vision for the future is as follows.

  • Become a leading tipper manufacturer in volume by producing 200 tippers/ month by end of 2007.
  • Supply tippers throughout the nation and support it with excellent and reliable service back up.
  • Introduce newer technology for manufacturing tippers.
  • Bring 2 new designs for tipper manufacturing.
  • Tie up with an international player for tipper design and production processes etc.
  • Get into speciality application like bulkers and cement mixtures.
