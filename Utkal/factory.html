Factory

Our factory is located at Jamshedpur in the state of Jharkhand. An industrial town, Jamshedpur is home to Tata Steel and Tata Motors, world renowned companies from the Tata Group. Jamshedpur, is 320 kms from the city of Kolkata and is accessible by rail and road transport.

Utkal Automobiles is spread over a large area of 16 acres and has 180000 sq ft of covered area making it one of the largest body building plants in India. Total direct and indirect employment for the year ended 2006 stood at 400 workers. The factory is self sustained and has modern infrastructure and machines to support its body building activities.
