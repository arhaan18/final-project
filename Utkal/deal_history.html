History

Utkal is an experienced stalwart in dealership operations where it has been involved in since 1956. Business operations started in the eastern state of Orissa where the company has now completed 50 years. During this long journey many products and services have been added. What started as an operation selling commercial vehicles now spans an entire product range from commercial vehicles to utility vehicles, cars and two wheelers. Sales for the year ended 2007 included 2500 units of cars and utlity vehicles and 1000 two wheelers making Utkal a Rs 150 crore company in sales turnover.

With a widespread network spanning 4 cities in Orissa, Utkal has successfully established and nurtured many brands and products in Orissa and has earned tremendous goodwill in the state. This has resulted in Utkal becoming the top dealer for Mahindra, Hyundai and Yamaha in the state of Orissa and a market leader for each of the manufacturer it represents.
