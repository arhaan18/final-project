About Us

Utkal Automobiles Limited was established in 1955 in the town of Jamshedpur, located in the eastern part of India, 350 kms from the port city of Kolkata. What began as a small business dealing in commercial vehicles has grown manifold and now spans a state of the art manufacturing...


 
History Dealerships

Utkal began manufacturing operations in Jamshedpur in 1968 and began supplies to Tata Motors and State Transport Units across the country. The company became synonymous with quality and design and soon Utkal’s buses, tippers and trucks, were seen across the country.
